About CarpentryCon 2018

  • What is CarpentryCon?

    CarpentryCon is the key community-building and networking event in Software and Data Carpentry's annual calendar of activities. This event will bring together new and more experienced community members to share knowledge, network and develop new skills. The conference theme is "Building Globally, Connecting Locally".
